Snake game

  • The game consists of a snake moving around a grid
  • The objective of the game is to eat as much food as possible and grow the snake.
  • The snake starts as a dot on the grid.
  • The snake can move in four directions: up, down, left, and right.
  • The snake moves continuously in the direction it is currently facing.
  • The player controls the direction of the snake using arrow keys
  • The snake cannot move in the opposite direction of its current movement, as this would result in collision with itself.
  • The snake can collide with the boundaries of the grid, which results in the end of the game.
  • The snake can collide with itself, which also results in the end of the game.
  • Food items, represented as dots appear at random positions on the grid
  • When the snake's head touches a food item, the snake grows longer by one segment.
  • Food can be either regular fruit which is worth 1 point or "super" fruit which is worth 10 points
  • "Super" fruit dissapears every 10 seconds and appears at a different location
  • The game continues until the snake collides with a boundary or itself.
  • Using the menu below, you can choose difficulty which affects speed of the snake's movement and map size which determines grid's width and height
